About Us

Sweet Treats by Khay is a home-based cake shop offering beautifully crafted baked goods for every occasion. From cupcakes and cookies to celebration cakes, each treat is made with care and creativity. The shop also accepts customization requests to match any theme, preference, or special event. Whether it's a birthday, anniversary, or just a craving for something sweet—Sweet Treats by Khay is ready to deliver delightful desserts made with love.

How It All Started

Sweet Treats by Khay began as a simple passion project during quiet weekends spent experimenting in the kitchen. What started as baking for family and friends quickly blossomed into something more when the joy these homemade treats brought to others became undeniable. Encouraged by their support and inspired by the happiness sweet creations could bring, Khay turned her love for baking into a small business—built on heart, creativity, and a sprinkle of sugar.

“Baking is love made edible.”
