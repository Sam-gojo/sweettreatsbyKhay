What kind of products do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of baked goods including cupcakes, cakes, cookies, and customized cakes for all occasions.

Do you accept customized cake orders?

Yes! Just send your preferred design, theme, flavor, and date needed.

How many days in advance should I place my order?

2–3 days for regular items, 5–7 days for custom cakes.

Where are you located?

We are based in Mabalacat, Pampanga. Pickup and delivery options are available.

Do you deliver?

Yes, with delivery fees based on your location. Details confirmed after order.

What flavors are available?

Chocolate, vanilla, mocha, red velvet, ube, and more. Ask us for the full list!

What are your payment options?

Cash on delivery, GCash, Maya, and bank transfer. Payment details are shared upon confirmation.

Can I cancel or change my order?

Yes, with at least 24 hours notice. Custom cakes may not be eligible for cancellation.

Do you offer bulk orders or party packages?

Yes! We cater for events like birthdays, weddings, and more. Contact us for packages.

Do you offer same-day orders?

Advance notice is preferred, but message us to check if we have items ready.
